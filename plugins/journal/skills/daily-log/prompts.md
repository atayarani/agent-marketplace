# Daily-log coaching prompts

Static fallback prompts for the AM and PM bookends. Pick the entry indexed to the user's local day-of-week — Monday = 1, Tuesday = 2, …, Sunday = 7.

## AM (morning intention)

1. **Monday** — What's the one thing today would feel incomplete without?
2. **Tuesday** — What are you avoiding that you could move on today?
3. **Wednesday** — Whose attention or energy do you need to protect today?
4. **Thursday** — What would make today feel meaningful?
5. **Friday** — What can future-you thank you for today?
6. **Saturday** — What do you actually want to do today?
7. **Sunday** — What kind of person are you trying to be this week?

## PM (evening reflection)

The PM sub-flow prefers a **body-aware question** generated from today's captures (see `SKILL.md` → "Body-aware question principles"). Use these static prompts as a fallback only when the body is empty or too sparse to anchor a question.

1. **Monday** — What worked, and what got in the way?
2. **Tuesday** — Did your morning intention hold up? Why or why not?
3. **Wednesday** — What made today feel heavy or light?
4. **Thursday** — What did you learn today that you didn't know yesterday?
5. **Friday** — What from this week is worth carrying forward?
6. **Saturday** — What was something good today that you almost missed?
7. **Sunday** — What do you want to leave behind from this week?
